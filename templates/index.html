About

What is it about!

With the great increase in credit card transactions, credit card fraud has become a major problem in recent years. In Modern day, the fraud is one of the major causes of great financial losses, not only for governments and banks but also the clients are affected.

This project intends to detect 100\% of fraudulent transactions and to minimize the incorrect fraud transactions, in machine learning it is considered as a classification problem.
